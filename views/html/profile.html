Leave a critique for the artist











Tips for how to critique

  • You do not need to be a professional art critic to participate.
  • Be respectful and courteous to the artists work.
  • Be specific about the piece of artwork that you are critiquing.
  • Comments or questions must pertain to artwork ONLY.
  • No slander, inappropriate comments, or personal attacks tolerated.
  • Use your best judgement! There are no right or wrong answers.
